About Us

About Tropical Roots Exteriors

At Tropical Roots Exteriors, we are passionate about transforming outdoor spaces into exceptional living areas that reflect your lifestyle and enhance your home's value. We are experts in designing and creating beautiful, functional exteriors, and we specialize in pergolas, pavilions, durable composite decks, and fully-equipped outdoor kitchens. Our commitment to quality craftsmanship ensures that every project we undertake is built to last, providing you with a space you'll love for years to come.

Based in Brevard County for over three decades, we know Florida lifestyle, while working with our clients to bring your custom vision to life. We tailor each project to your specific needs, style, and preferences. From the initial design consultation to the final installation, we pride ourselves on our attention to detail, exceptional customer service, and dedication to creating outdoor living spaces that are not only beautiful but also functional and environmentally friendly. Let Tropical Roots Exteriors turn your backyard into your personal oasis!
